Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Today we will be making delicious chocolate chip cookies! They are good for any occasion.

This recipe will be easy to follow and are best enjoyed with a glass of milk.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of softened butter
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • A cup of chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees farenheit.
  2. Mix the butter, eggs, and vanilla extract in a bowl until the mixture is a creamy consistency.
  3. Add in chocolate chips.
  4. Create two inch balls and place them two inches apart on a baking sheet.
  5. Bake the cookies for 8 minutes until they are lightly brown.
  6. Let the cookies cool on a cooling tray before eating.
