Churros

Description

Welcome! Today we will be baking delicious churros!

You remember these delicious treats from the fair, but today you will make them in your own home!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of water
  • 6 tablespoons of bytter
  • 2 table spoons of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • Vegetable oil
  • Cinnamon sugar

Steps

  1. Add water, butter, and sugar in a saucepan until boiled. Turn off heat and add vanilla.
  2. When mixture is cool, add eggs and beat until combined. Add mixture to a piping bag.
  3. Add 6 inches of rope of mixture into large boiling pot halfway filled with oil.
  4. Fry mixture until golden brown.
  5. Remove churros and immediately roll churros in Cinnamon sugar.
  6. Let churros cool before consuming.
