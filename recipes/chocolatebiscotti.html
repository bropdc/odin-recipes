Chocolate Biscotti

Description

Today we will be making delicious chocolate biscotti! They are good for any occasion.

This recipe will be easy to follow and are best enjoyed with a cup of joe.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1/2 cup of dark cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 6 tablespoons salted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees farenheit.
  2. Mix the flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt in a bowl.
  3. In another bowl, beat butter and sugar using an electric mixer until fluffy. Then add eggs and vanilla extract.
  4. Add flour mixture and chocolate chips to the egg mixture. You should have a stiff mix of dough.
  5. Create twelve inch logs onto a baking sheet and bake for 35 minutes.
  6. Let the biscotti cool on a cooling tray before eating.
